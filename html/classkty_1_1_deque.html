Detailed Description

template<typename T, typename Alloc = Allocator<Sizes::alloc_size, Sizes::alloc_block_size>, typename GetAllocFunc = decltype(get_alloc)>
class kty::Deque< T, Alloc, GetAllocFunc >

Double-ended queue. Provides quick insertion and deletion at both ends, but at the expense of slow random access. Implemented as a circular doubly linked list with a dummy head node.
